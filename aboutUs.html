Principal's Message

Government Polytechnic Gorakhpur is one of the most prestigious institutions of learning in Gorakhpur district in uttar Pradesh and is running with innovation, creativity, human intelligence and patience. This institute provides technical education and technical education is the backbone of every nation.The main focus of the institution is to empower students with sound knowledge, wisdom , experience, and training both at the academic level of Engineering and in the highly competitive global industrial market. This institute has well equipped labs, Computer centre, library, talented and dedicated faculty members to help students in attaining high standards in academics and professional skills.

-Er. Ashis Kumar Mishra(Principal)

(GOVERNMENT POLYTECHNIC GORAKHPUR, Gorakhpur(U.P.))

Our Mission


Our mission is to impart quality education to achieve excellence in teaching learning and research and to provide skill oriented training to students to meet the need of the industry and society. To promote indigenous technology to replace the imported technology with the aim to implement “make in India” motto in collaboration with industry institute introduction and partnership.



Our Vision


To achieve and maintain the status of reputed institute of excellence in technical education & produce world class technocrats with competent skills in various technologies for serving the society.
